Company Details on Right Side of Header

Changes position of Company details and display on Right Side of Header

By default Odoo displays Company details like Name, logo, address and other details on the left side of invoice

Sometimes in a printed filing system, this might cover the company details in large folder

This module Changes position of Company details and display on Right Side of Header

Before

Default company details are on left

After

This module configures it to the right

Lists

Calendar

Header is updated on all reports like Invoice, Quotations, Purchase Order etc.
